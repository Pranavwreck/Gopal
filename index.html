About Us

Chairman - Pranav S

I am Pranav S from Chennai pursuing my 3rd year of ECE in Sri Sairam Engineering College. I am very interested in working for the development of my country & upliftment of the poor & the needy people. In this role, I identified a need for a new system for blind people and in that connection, I worked with my team to provide an imaginative solution and finally we designed a "Smart blind stick for the visually impaired". Ours is a low cost product. Since there is a huge ratio of people below poverty, this low cost product will be very much useful for them and even others.So that even visually impaired people could live a life beyond dark
